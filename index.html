How to check your Vanilla gift card balance online



Checking your Vanilla gift card balance online is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. Here’s how:

1. Visit the Vanilla gift card website at www.vanillagift.com.
2. Click on the “Check Balance” button located at the top of the page.
3. Enter your 16-digit card number, expiration date, and CVV code (found on the back of the card).
4. Click on the “Check Balance” button again.
5. Once you’ve completed these steps, your current balance will be displayed on the screen. If you have multiple Vanilla gift cards, you can repeat this process for each card.
